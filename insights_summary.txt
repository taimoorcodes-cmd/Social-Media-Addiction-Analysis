Social Media Addiction Analysis
Key Business Insights
Author: Taimoor
================================================

1. USAGE VS ADDICTION (STRONG)
------------------------------
Heavy Users    = 67.00 avg addiction
Medium Users   = 60.77 avg addiction
Low Users      = 42.95 avg addiction
Finding: Heavy social media users have 
significantly higher addiction scores.

2. DOPAMINE VS ADDICTION (STRONGEST)
------------------------------
High Dopamine  = 68.66 avg addiction
Medium Dopamine= 62.09 avg addiction
Low Dopamine   = 46.94 avg addiction
Finding: Dopamine dependency is the 
strongest predictor of addiction.

3. SLEEP QUALITY VS ADDICTION (STRONG)
------------------------------
Low Addiction  = 7.30 sleep quality
Medium         = 6.81 sleep quality
High Addiction = 5.83 sleep quality
Finding: Higher addiction levels are 
associated with worse sleep quality.

4. TIKTOK VS INSTAGRAM
------------------------------
TikTok Heavy   = 59.42 avg addiction
Instagram Heavy= 57.21 avg addiction
Finding: TikTok users show slightly 
higher addiction than Instagram users.

5. TOP COUNTRIES
------------------------------
Netherlands = 116 high addiction users
Angola      = 107 high addiction users
Russia      = 105 high addiction users
Finding: Netherlands leads in high 
addiction users globally.

6. MENTAL HEALTH RISK
------------------------------
High Addiction = 45.80 avg MHRI
Medium         = 40.53 avg MHRI
Low Addiction  = 38.98 avg MHRI
Finding: Higher addiction is strongly 
linked to higher mental health risk.

7. AGE GROUP ANALYSIS (WEAK)
------------------------------
Adult       = 58.75 avg addiction
Older Adult = 58.56 avg addiction
Teen        = 58.42 avg addiction
Young Adult = 58.33 avg addiction
Finding: Age has very weak relationship 
with addiction score.

8. SLEEP HOURS VS ADDICTION (WEAK)
------------------------------
Low Sleep    = 58.73 avg addiction
High Sleep   = 58.59 avg addiction
Medium Sleep = 58.43 avg addiction
Finding: Sleep hours alone do not 
strongly predict addiction.

================================================
SUMMARY FOR MANAGER
================================================
The strongest predictors of social media 
addiction are:
1. Dopamine dependency (difference: 21.72)
2. Social media usage level (difference: 24.05)
3. Sleep quality (difference: 1.47)

Age, sleep hours, and impulsivity showed 
weak relationships with addiction score.
